# Structure Review

## Purpose

Review the organizational structure of a document, proposing cuts, reorganization, and simplification to make the document more concise while maintaining comprehensibility.

## How to Execute

1. **Analyze document structure** — Heading hierarchy, paragraph organization, information flow.

2. **Check for:**
   - Whether the structure depth is appropriate (too deep? too shallow?)
   - Whether the information order matches reader needs (important things first?)
   - Whether any sections can be merged
   - Whether any sections can be removed without affecting understanding
   - Whether headings accurately reflect their content
   - Whether a table of contents is needed (for long documents)

3. **Produce reorganization suggestions** — Propose a new structural outline with rationale.
