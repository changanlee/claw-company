# Step 4: Converge Solutions

**Progress: Step 4 of 5**

## Objective

Converge ideas from the divergent phase into 2-3 actionable proposals with pros/cons analysis.

## Execution Rules

- Read the entire step file before acting
- Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- Do not skip or merge steps

## Instructions

### 1. Classify & Filter

1. Group all ideas by theme.
2. Remove duplicates and infeasible ideas.
3. Identify the most promising directions.

### 2. Integrate Proposals

Consolidate filtered ideas into **2-3 proposals**, each including:

- Proposal name and core concept
- Strengths analysis
- Weaknesses analysis
- Required resources
- Feasibility assessment

### 3. Present to Chairman

Present proposals one at a time, asking only **one question** at a time, waiting for a response before continuing.

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] Ideas classified and filtered
- [ ] Consolidated into 2-3 proposals
- [ ] Each proposal includes pros/cons analysis
