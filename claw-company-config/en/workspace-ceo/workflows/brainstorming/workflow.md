# CEO Brainstorming Workflow

## Overview

CEO (Nova) hosts a cross-department brainstorming session, inviting relevant executives to participate, using structured thinking techniques to explore topics, converging into actionable proposals, and reporting to the Chairman.

## Prerequisites

- Topic or request raised by the Chairman
- `{{INSTALL_DIR}}/shared/brain-methods.csv` (brainstorming technique library)

## Step Overview

| Step | Name | Description | Approval |
|------|------|-------------|----------|
| 01 | Define Topic | Define the topic + invite relevant executives | — |
| 02 | Select Technique | Choose thinking technique mode | — |
| 03 | Execute Technique | Execute technique, guide divergent thinking | — |
| 04 | Converge Solutions | Converge ideas into 2-3 proposals | — |
| 05 | Summary Report | Produce summary and report to Chairman | — |
