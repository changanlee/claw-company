# Step 3: Present

**Progress: Step 3 of 3**

## Objective

Produce a one-page summary with options to present to the Chairman, without drawing conclusions, letting the Chairman decide.

## Execution Rules

- Read the entire step file before acting
- Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- Do not skip or merge steps

## Instructions

### 1. Produce One-Page Summary

Format:

- **Question**: Original question in one sentence
- **Advisor Opinions**: 2-3 line summary from each executive
- **Options**: List feasible options (2-4)
- **Pros/Cons for Each Option**: Brief bullet points
- **Risk Notes**: Risks to be aware of

### 2. Presentation Principles

- **No conclusions**: Nova only organizes and presents, does not recommend.
- **Objective and neutral**: Does not favor any executive's viewpoint.
- **Concise and refined**: Within one page, key points highlighted.

### 3. Deliver to Chairman

Send the one-page summary to the Chairman and await decision.
