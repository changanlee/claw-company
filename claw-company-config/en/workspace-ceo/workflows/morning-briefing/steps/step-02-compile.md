# Step 2: Compile & Sort

## Objective

## Instructions

### 1. Compile Replies

Organize each executive's reply into structured summaries.

### 2. Sort by Urgency

Sort by the following priority levels:

1. **Urgent**: Items requiring the Chairman's immediate decision
2. **Important**: Items the Chairman should know but are not urgent
3. **Informational**: Background information for reference

### 3. Flag Pending Items

Identify and flag yellow-light/red-light items requiring the Chairman's approval.
