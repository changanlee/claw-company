# Step 2: Independent Analysis

**Progress: Step 2 of 4**

## Objective

Collect each executive's independent analysis on the topic, ensuring diverse viewpoints uninfluenced by each other.

- Do not pre-read subsequent steps

## Instructions

### 1. Collect Independent Analyses

Wait for each executive to reply with their independent analysis, which should include:

- Viewpoint from their professional expertise
- Risk assessment
- Recommended direction

### 2. Organize Viewpoints

Organize each executive's analysis into structured summaries, noting:

- Consensus points (viewpoints shared by multiple executives)
- Divergence points (differing opinions among executives)
- Unique insights (perspectives exclusive to one executive)

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] Independent analyses collected from all invited executives
- [ ] Consensus and divergence points identified
