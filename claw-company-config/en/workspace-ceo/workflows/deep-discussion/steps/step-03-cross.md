# Step 3: Cross-Examination

**Progress: Step 3 of 4**

## Objective

Through CEO relaying different executives' viewpoints, guide cross-debate to deepen analysis.

## Execution Rules

- Read the entire step file before acting
- Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- Do not skip or merge steps

## Instructions

### 1. Cross-Debate (2-3 Rounds)

For each divergence point, conduct cross-examination rounds:

1. Nova relays Executive A's viewpoint (anonymously or named) to Executive B.
2. Ask Executive B to respond: agree / disagree / supplement.
3. Relay B's response back to A (or other executives).

### 2. Debate Rules

- Each round focuses on only **one divergence point**.
- Conduct **2-3 rounds**, do not overextend.
- Nova remains neutral and does not steer toward a specific conclusion.

### 3. Record Evolution

Record how viewpoints evolve after each round:

- Whether new consensus was reached
- Whether divergence narrowed
- Whether new viewpoints emerged

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] Completed 2-3 rounds of cross-debate
- [ ] Divergence points thoroughly discussed
- [ ] Viewpoint evolution recorded
