# Step 2: Evaluate

**Progress: Step 2 of 3**

## Objective

Conduct a preliminary assessment of candidate opportunities.

## Execution Rules

- Read the entire step file before acting
- Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- Do not skip or merge steps

## Instructions

### 1. Quick Assessment

For each candidate opportunity, assess:

- Potential return (%)
- Risk level (Low/Medium/High)
- Time sensitivity (Urgent/Normal)
- Confidence level (High/Medium/Low)

### 2. Classify

- **High confidence**: High potential return, manageable risk, time-sensitive
- **Medium confidence**: Worth tracking, but not urgent
- **Low confidence**: Record only, monitor

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] All candidates assessed
- [ ] Classified by confidence level
