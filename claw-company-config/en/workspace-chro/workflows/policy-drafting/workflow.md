# CHRO Policy Drafting Workflow

## Overview

CHRO (Harper) identifies policy needs and triggers, researches relevant contexts and best practices, drafts policy documents, submits for CEO review and CAO compliance check (tripartite checks and balances), and finally escalates to the Chairman for approval.

## Prerequisites

- Clear policy need (create/modify/delete)
- Trigger reason (incident, audit finding, organizational change, etc.)

## Steps Overview

| Step | Name | Description | Approval |
|------|------|-------------|----------|
| 01 | Identify Need | Confirm policy need, clarify trigger reason | — |
| 02 | Research | Research relevant contexts and best practices | — |
| 03 | Draft Policy | Draft policy document | — |
| 04 | Tripartite Review | CEO review + CAO compliance check | Yellow (CEO) |
| 05 | Submit for Approval | Submit revised policy to Chairman | Red (Chairman) |

## Approval Gates

| Decision Item | Level | Handling |
|--------------|-------|---------|
| Policy draft review | Yellow | Submit to CEO review + CAO compliance check |
| Policy enactment | Red | Escalate to Chairman via CEO |
