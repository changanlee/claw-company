# Step 3: Draft Policy

**Progress: Step 3 of 5**

## Goal

Based on research results, draft a complete policy document.

## Execution Rules

- 📖 Read the entire step file before taking action
- 🚫 Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- 🚫 Do not skip steps

## Instructions

### 1. Use Template

Read `{{INSTALL_DIR}}/workspace-chro/templates/policy-draft.md` and draft according to the template structure.

### 2. Required Policy Elements

- **Policy Name**: Clear, concise
- **Purpose**: Why this policy is needed
- **Scope**: Which Agents, which scenarios
- **Rules**: Specific rules and requirements
- **Enforcement**: How to implement, who monitors
- **Approval Level**: Which approval tier applies
- **Exception Handling**: How to handle special cases
- **Effective Date**: When the policy takes effect

### 3. Writing Principles

- Use clear language, avoid ambiguous wording
- Each rule should be independently understandable
- Maintain consistency with company-rules.md
- Consider impact on each Agent's workflows

### 4. Self-Review

- Does the policy address the triggering issue
- Is there over-constraint (impacting efficiency)
- Does it conflict with existing policies
- Is the enforcement mechanism feasible

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] Policy document completed using template
- [ ] All required elements included
- [ ] Self-review passed
