# Step 5: Produce Recommendation

**Progress: Step 5 of 5**

## Objective

Synthesize all analyses and produce a purchase recommendation supported by data.

## Execution Rules

- Read the entire step file before acting
- Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- Do not skip or merge steps

## Instructions

### 1. Produce Recommendation

Recommendations fall into three categories:

- **Recommend Purchase**: Sufficient budget, clear need, good timing
- **Recommend Deferral**: Tight budget, can wait for promotions, not urgent
- **Recommend Alternative**: A more cost-effective alternative exists

### 2. Attach Data

Each recommendation includes:

- Budget impact data
- Historical comparison data
- Needs assessment results

### 3. Submit to CEO

Send the analysis report to CEO, who decides whether to escalate to the Chairman (Yellow).
