# Step 4: Needs Assessment

**Progress: Step 4 of 5**

## Objective

Assess the priority level of the purchase need.

## Execution Rules

- Read the entire step file before acting
- Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- Do not skip or merge steps

## Instructions

### 1. Need Classification

Classify the purchase need into:

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Essential | Life/work necessity; not purchasing would have negative impact |
| Want | Improves quality of life but not essential |
| Deferrable | Can wait for better timing or price |

### 2. Assessment Factors

- Estimated usage frequency
- Feasibility of alternatives
- Timing factors (promotions, seasonality)

- [ ] Assessment factors analyzed
