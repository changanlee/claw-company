# Step 3: Accommodation

**Progress: Step 3 of 6**

## Goal

Collect accommodation options based on destination, duration, budget, and preferences.

## Execution Rules

- 📖 Read the entire step file before taking action
- 🚫 Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- 🚫 Do not skip steps

## Instructions

### 1. Search Accommodation Options

Based on destination area and check-in/check-out dates, search available accommodations:
- Hotels: star rating, price, location
- B&B/Airbnb: type, reviews, location
- Other: hostels, serviced apartments

### 2. Organize into 2-3 Plans

Each plan should include:
- Accommodation name and type
- Nightly rate / total cost
- Location (distance to major attractions/transport hubs)
- Highlights and review summary
- Cancellation policy

### 3. Rank Recommendations

Rank based on Chairman's preferences, budget, and location convenience.

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] Searched available accommodation options
- [ ] Organized into 2-3 plans with prices
- [ ] Provided ranked recommendations
