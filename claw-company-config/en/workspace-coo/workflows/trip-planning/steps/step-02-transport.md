## Instructions

### 1. Search Transport Options

Based on origin, destination, and dates, search available transport methods:
- Flights: schedules, airlines, fares
- Train/rail: schedules, fares, travel time
- Self-drive: route, fuel cost estimate, travel time
- Other: bus, ferry, etc.

### 2. Organize into 2-3 Plans

Each plan should include:
- Transport method and specific schedule/route
- Estimated cost (round trip)
- Travel time
- Pros and cons (comfort, flexibility, price)

### 3. Rank Recommendations

Rank recommendations based on Chairman's preferences and budget, indicating the top recommendation with reasoning.
