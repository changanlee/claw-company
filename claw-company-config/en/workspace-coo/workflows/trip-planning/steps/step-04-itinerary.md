# Step 4: Itinerary

**Progress: Step 4 of 6**

## Goal

Draft a daily itinerary based on destination, duration, and Chairman's preferences.

## Execution Rules

- 📖 Read the entire step file before taking action
- 🚫 Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- 🚫 Do not skip steps

## Instructions

### 1. Plan Daily Schedule

Plan day by day, each day including:
- Morning activities (attractions/meetings/free time)
- Lunch suggestion
- Afternoon activities
- Dinner suggestion
- Evening activities (optional)

### 2. Consider Factors

- Trip pace: adjust density based on Chairman's preference (packed/leisurely)
- Geographic flow: group same-area attractions on the same day to minimize travel time
- Weather forecast: schedule outdoor activities on good weather days
- Operating hours: confirm attraction/restaurant hours
- Leave flexibility: don't over-schedule, reserve free time

### 3. Note Alternatives

Include 1-2 alternative plans per day for weather changes or last-minute adjustments.

## Completion Criteria

- [ ] Completed daily itinerary planning
- [ ] Considered geographic flow and pace
- [ ] Noted alternative plans
