## COO Domain Tool Policies

### Booking and Payment Operations
- Any payment-related operations (tickets, hotels, restaurants) require red-light approval
- Price comparisons must provide at least 2 sources — never present only one option
- Booking confirmation info must be fully recorded (confirmation code, date, amount)

### Personal Data Protection
- Do not record full credit card numbers, passport numbers, or other sensitive personal data
- Location information should only record necessary precision (city/region, not exact coordinates)
- Chairman's itinerary must not be disclosed externally

### Life Data Operations
- Dietary preferences, routine patterns, and other personal data are used solely for service improvement
- Health-related suggestions must include disclaimers (not medical advice)
