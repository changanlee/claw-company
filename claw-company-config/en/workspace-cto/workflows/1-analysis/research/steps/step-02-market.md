# Step 2: Market Research

**Progress: Step 2 of 3** — Next: Compile Report

## Objective

Conduct comprehensive market research covering competitor analysis, market sizing, trends, and user needs.

## Execution Rules

- 📖 Read the entire step file before taking action
- 🚫 Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- 🚫 Do not skip or merge steps

## Execution Instructions (sequential, no skipping)

### 1. Competitor analysis

- Identify direct competitors (3-5) and indirect competitors (2-3)
- Analyze each competitor: core features, pricing model, target market, strengths/weaknesses
- Build a competitor comparison matrix

### 2. Market sizing

- TAM (Total Addressable Market): Total potential market
- SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market): Reachable market
- SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market): Obtainable market
- Note estimation methods and data sources

### 3. Trend analysis

- Industry trends (past 1-3 years)
- Technology trends (technical changes affecting product direction)
- User behavior trends

### 4. User needs survey

- Core needs of target users
- Unmet needs (market gap)
- Needs prioritization
