# Step 4: Validate and Report

**Progress: Step 4 of 4** — Final step

## Goal

Validate the Sprint status document for completeness, ensure all Stories are included, and produce a planning report.

## Execution Rules

- 📖 Read the entire step file before taking action
- 🚫 Do not pre-read subsequent steps
- 🚫 Do not skip or merge steps

## Execution Instructions

### 1. Completeness Validation

Check each item:

- Every Story from every Epic appears in the Sprint status document
- No duplicate Stories
- All required fields are filled for every Story
- No circular dependencies

### 2. Consistency Check

- Story titles in the Sprint status document match the Epic originals
- Story count matches the count defined in Epics
- Ordering is reasonable (dependencies first)

### 3. Produce Planning Report

Compile a Sprint planning summary for CTO:

- Sprint overview: Total Story count, number of source Epics
- Story distribution: Count by Epic
- Dependency chains: Critical path dependencies
- Risk flags: Oversized Stories, long dependency chains

## Completion Criteria

- ✅ Completeness validation passed
- ✅ Consistency check passed
- ✅ Planning report produced

## Next Step

This is the final step. Report the planning summary to CTO. Sprint is ready.
